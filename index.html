Welcome to Agrolab503

Agrolab503 is dedicated to agricultural research, teaching, and outreach. We investigate sustainable crop systems, plant physiology, and agroecology, collaborating across departments and with local farmers.

Lab Introduction

Our lab focuses on applied agricultural science: optimizing crop production under changing climates, improving soil health, and developing integrated pest management strategies. We combine field experiments, lab work, and data science to translate research into practice.

Related Work

  • Crop rotation effects on soil microbiome and yield (ongoing field trials)
  • Physiological responses of major cultivars to drought stress
  • Assessment of on-farm biodiversity and ecosystem services

Lab Location

Department of Agriculture, National Chung Hsing University (NCHU)
